Medmart - Platform for buying and selling medicines

Introduction

Actually, Medmart is one of the design competitions that I participated in via the Instagram platform. This design is designed to make it easier for you to get medicines quickly and safely from anywhere. With a simple design and elegant colors, this application offers an exceptional and intuitive user experience.

Our user interface was designed with a focus on simplicity and ease of navigation. The colors used in the app are carefully selected to create a display that is not only visually appealing but also easy on the eyes, ensuring a pleasant shopping experience.

We also implemented a search feature and well-organized categories, allowing you to find the right product quickly. Each product is accompanied by a detailed description, dosage, and other important information, so you can make a sound and informed purchasing decision.

The security of your transactions is guaranteed with a secure and encrypted payment system. Besides, we offers a variety of flexible payment options, including credit cards, e-wallets and bank transfers. Download Medmart today and experience the convenience of purchasing medicines in a modern, fast, and convenient way stylish, anytime and anywhere. What are the features?

  • Medicine search feature

    • This feature allows users to easily search for the medication they need. Users can enter drug names, categories, or disease symptoms to find a list of relevant medicine. This sophisticated search feature is designed to provide fast and accurate results, making it easier for users to navigate the wide selection of medications available in the application.

  • View medicine categories

    • In this feature, medications are grouped by type of use, such as analgesics, antibiotics, or vitamins and supplements. It helps users to easily browse and select drugs based on specific categories according to their needs. These categories are arranged systematically to make it easier for users to find the right product.

  • Favorite product

    • This feature allows users to save medicines that they frequently buy or that they want to buy in the future into their favorites list. This way, users can quickly access a list of these medications without needing to search again in the future, simplifying the repeat purchasing process.

  • Cart Features

    • The basket feature functions as a place for users to save their choice of medicines before making payment. Users can add a number of medications to their cart while continuing to shop, and then edit the quantity or remove medications from the cart before completing the transaction.

  • Transaction

    • This feature manages the payment process and purchase confirmation. Users can choose their preferred payment method, starting from payment via credit card, e-wallet, or bank transfer. The application also ensures transaction security by using the latest encryption and data protection to protect users' personal and financial information.

  • Product purchase history

    • This feature allows users to view the history of all purchases they have previously made in the app. This is useful for tracking purchases, managing budgets, or for medical purposes where users need to know the history of medications they have taken.
