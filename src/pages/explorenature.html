ExploreNature - Platform providing mountain tourism

Introduction

ExploreNature is micro challenge project that I worked on at Infinite Learning MSIB batch 6. Where this project only reached the prototype stage. ExploreNature is The definitive platform for mountain climbers in Indonesia. Here, you can find all the information you need to plan your hike perfectly. At ExploreNature we provide a comprehensive list of mountains throughout Indonesia, complete with details of the height, location, and difficulty level of each climbing route. Not only that, each mountain comes with a detailed climbing route that includes important information such as climbing duration, rest points and proper equipment suggestions.

Our discussion forum opens a space for climbers to exchange information, tips and experiences. From beginners to experienced climbers, everyone can participate and share stories or ask questions directly to the community. You can also get personal recommendations from other climbers about the best routes and climbing preparation tips. We know the importance of easy and fast access to information, which is why our site design is responsive and easy to navigate on a variety of devices. Whether you access it from a laptop, tablet or smartphone, all the information you need is at your fingertips.

So, what are you waiting for? Register today and start planning your dream climb with ExploreNature the best friend of mountain climbers in Indonesia!

What are the features?

  • Information on mountains in East Java

    • This feature provides complete and detailed information about various mountains located in East Java. Users can access descriptions of mountain height, geographical location, track difficulty level, weather conditions, as well as travel tips and recommendations suitable for that mountain. This information is very useful for climbers to better plan their climbs and ensure safety during the trek.

  • List of mountain tourism in East Java

    • In this feature, users can see a comprehensive list of mountains in East Java. Each entry in this list is linked to a detail page that provides further information about each mountain. This makes it easier for users to explore and choose their next climbing destination based on their personal preferences such as the level of difficulty, natural beauty, or uniqueness of the mountain.

  • Discussion forum between fellow climbers

    • This forum is an online community where climbers can share experiences, exchange tips, and discuss various aspects related to mountain climbing. Users can ask questions, answer questions, or simply share stories and photos from their hikes. This forum is also an ideal place to make new friends who share your interest in mountaineering, or even plan a climb together as a group.

  • Sales and purchase of climbing equipment

    • This feature makes ExplorNature a one-stop source for all climbing equipment needs. This platform provides e-commerce facilities where users can buy or sell climbing equipment, both new and used. The products available include essential equipment such as backpacks, trekking shoes, tents, sleeping bags, and more. With this feature, climbers can easily get all the equipment they need for their climb, often at competitive prices.
