About me

As someone who really likes learning about technology, I will continue to look for ways to deepen and expand his knowledge in various technical fields and the latest innovations. From the various fields I have explored, I found that I have a strong interest in web design and development. Therefore, I decided to become a frontend developer because this profession allows me to combine creativity and technical skills in creating effective and interesting user experiences.

Currently, I'm still in the beginning stages as a developer, with a basic understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap. I'm in the process of learning Tailwind, React.js more interactive web applications and Node.js to understand more about server side development. Apart from that, I am also quite fluent in PHP and already have experience using the Laravel framework. By the way I also like playing games and watching anime.
