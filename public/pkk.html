E-PKK - PKK data reporting website in Nganjuk district

Introduction

Welcome to E-PKK Kabupaten Nganjuk an innovative web platform specifically designed to help and simplify the process of reporting and managing PKK (Family Empowerment and Welfare) data in Nganjuk Regency. The platform provides efficient solutions for data collection, analysis and reporting, ensuring all information related to the PKK program can be easily accessed by various stakeholders.

E-PKK Kabupaten Nganjuk was developed with the aim of supporting PKK activities throughout Nganjuk Regency, providing a powerful but easy-to-use tool to increase administrative effectiveness and efficiency. With a user-friendly interface, this platform allows PKK cadres and administrators at sub-district to village levels to input, monitor and report data quickly and accurately.

What are the features?

  • Homepage

    • The main page of this website displays a general overview or important information regarding the PKK in Nganjuk Regency. Visitors can see highlights of the latest news, important announcements, as well as photo galleries from recent activities. Its intuitive design allows users to get up-to-date and relevant information quickly and easily.

  • Dashboard

    • The dashboard feature provides a quick view of important statistics including the number of incoming reports and the number of active users in the system. This helps admins monitor activity on the website and measure participation in PKK programs. This dashboard is equipped with various analytical tools that help in making strategic decisions based on data.

  • News

    • Admin can add the latest news regarding PKK activities or programs which will then appear on the homepage. This feature allows dynamic management of news content, where admins can post, edit and delete news as needed. This provides an important means of keeping members and the general public informed about the PKK's activities and achievements.

  • Announcement

    • This announcement feature is integrated with the PKK mobile application, ensuring that all important announcements can be accessed by users not only through the website but also through the application on their mobile devices. This includes announcements about upcoming events, policy changes, or other important information that needs to be communicated quickly to members and the public.

  • Gallery

    • The gallery contains images of PKK activities that have been uploaded via the mobile application. Admins have the task of managing these images, including approving, editing, or deleting inappropriate images before they appear on the homepage. This gallery aims to visualize the PKK's activities and achievements, providing visual evidence of the positive impact of their activities.

  • Reporting

    • Similar to galleries, workgroups contain reports received from mobile applications. Admins are in charge of managing these reports, which includes approving, editing, and deleting these reports before they are published. This feature ensures that only valid and quality reports are published, assisting in the documentation and evaluation of PKK programs.

With E-PKK kbabupaten Nganjuk the process of reporting and managing PKK data is not only faster and easier, but also more accurate and efficient. This platform is specifically designed to meet the dynamic needs of the PKK organization, empowering administrators and cadres with technology that supports their duties in helping the community. Start using E-PKK kbabupaten Nganjuk today and experience the ease of managing and reporting data to realize better and more organized empowerment and welfare of families in Nganjuk Regency.
